# Image Upload Guide

## üìÅ Where to Put Your Photos

Add your photos to these folders in VS Code:

### Required Images

1. **Hero Image** (main homepage image)
   - Location: `public/images/hero-main.jpg`
   - Photo: Youth at gym or cultural camp (powerful landscape shot)
   - Size: 1200 x 800px

2. **Brodie's Photo**
   - Location: `public/images/brodie.jpg`
   - Photo: Professional photo of Brodie
   - Size: 600 x 800px (portrait)

### Gallery Images

Add 6+ photos to `public/images/gallery/` with these names:

- `gym-session.jpg` - Youth working out at gym
- `cultural-camp.jpg` - Cultural camp on country
- `elder-teaching.jpg` - Elder sharing knowledge
- `group-activity.jpg` - Group activity or gathering
- `mentoring.jpg` - Brodie with youth
- `family-event.jpg` - Family engagement activity

Add more photos as: `gallery-1.jpg`, `gallery-2.jpg`, etc.
